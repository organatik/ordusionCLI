Project Preparation

This is the exploration phase. Our domain experts work with the clients to identify the requirements and document them with impeccable details. In our experience we have realized a successful project is made possible by starting with clear and precise requirement. We also create and refine the project plan, review the organization, resources while defining the logistics needed.

Business Blue Print

During Blueprint phase, we conduct Workshops with the users. At the conclusion of the phase the project plan will be re-evaluated based on the outcome of the workshops. Estimates and scope may have to be adjusted for changes of scope, if any.

Realization

Project Realization is the projectâ€™s configuration, development and testing phase. During this phase, the project team configures and creates all necessary code developments. Also in this phase, we will perform unit and integration testing of the completed configurations and developments to ensure end-to-end functionality. The phase concludes with the user testing and a Quality Assurance review of the testing results with a formal Go-No-Go decision.

Final Preparation

The purpose of this phase is to execute the cutover plan into production and to ensure readiness to go live. The Final Preparation phase also serves to resolve all crucial open issues. On successful completion of this phase, developed process is ready to Go Live.
